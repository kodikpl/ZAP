ZAPTV Information

Completely Free

Using **ZAPTV** is and will always be completely free for all users. We aim to provide a simple, centralized platform for discovering and watching publicly embeddable channels without any cost or subscription.

Important Disclaimer

ZAPTV does not host or stream any video content on its own servers. We function solely as an embed viewer. All channels and streams displayed on ZAPTV are embedded directly from third-party platforms such as YouTube, Twitch, Kick, VK, and other services that offer publicly available embed codes.

This means the content is streamed and controlled entirely by the original content creators and the respective third-party platforms.
